Thursday, August 20
Vivekananda Heritage & Chicago Highlights
  • 8:00 AM
    Breakfast Food
    Fuel up before a big day of walking.
  • 9:00 AM
    Vivekananda Way & Art Institute of Chicago 📍 Culture
    Walk to the Art Institute (~8 min). See Vivekananda Way 📍 — the honorary street commemorating Swami Vivekananda's 1893 Parliament of Religions address — then the Art Institute. Plan ~3 hours. Book timed tickets ahead.
  • Lunch
    The Gage 📍 Food
    Right across from the Art Institute on Michigan Avenue.
  • Afternoon
    Chicago Architecture River Cruise 📍 Tour
    The best way to see the city's skyline — 90 minutes on the river. Reserve tickets in advance.
  • After cruise
    Riverwalk & Magnificent Mile Fun
    Stroll the Chicago Riverwalk 📍, then Michigan Avenue and the Magnificent Mile 📍.
  • Evening
    360 CHICAGO Observation Deck 📍 Views
    Sweeping views from the John Hancock Center (or Willis Tower Skydeck 📍 if you prefer).
  • Dinner
    Italian or Indian Food
    Eataly Chicago 📍 · Beatrix 📍 · Quartino Ristorante 📍. For Indian: Rooh Chicago 📍 (modern Indian, West Loop) or India House 📍 (River North).
  • Overnight
    Overnight in Chicago 🌙
3Day
Friday, August 21
Shedd Aquarium, Navy Pier & FlyOver
  • Morning
    Shedd Aquarium 📍 Culture
    Recommended — pairs perfectly with the Navy Pier afternoon by the lake. Prefer dinosaurs? Swap in the Field Museum 📍 instead. Both sit on the Museum Campus 📍.
  • Lunch
    Near Museum Campus Food
    Grab a bite close to the museums before heading up to the pier.
  • Afternoon
    Navy Pier 📍 Fun
    Lakefront walk, shops, and boat rides (optional). A highlight for the kids.
  • ~30 min
    FlyOver Chicago 📍 Fun
    Right at Navy Pier — a thrilling flight-simulation ride that soars over Chicago's skyline and landmarks (wind, mist, and motion included). About 30 minutes. ~$28 per person (approx.; check current pricing). Great for all ages.
  • Optional
    Centennial Wheel 📍 Views
    If there's time and energy, ride the giant Ferris wheel for skyline views over the lake. ~$20 per person.
  • Evening
    Relax by the water Views
    Wind down at Ohio Street Beach 📍 or along the Riverwalk 📍.
  • Dinner
    Chicago specialty or Indian Food
    Small Cheval 📍 (burgers) · a classic Chicago deep-dish · or go Indian at Cumin 📍 or The Spice Room 📍. For South Indian vegetarian, Udupi Palace 📍 on Devon Ave is worth the trip.
  • Overnight
    Overnight in Chicago 🌙
