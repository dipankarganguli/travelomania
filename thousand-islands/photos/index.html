Open Google Photos

2
Open the album link
Tap: photos.app.goo.gl/W1zCAE1NW9KFy8Qz9 ↗ — it opens directly in Google Photos.
3
Tap the "+" or "Add photos" button
At the top of the album, tap "Add photos". This opens your phone's photo library.
4
Bulk select your trip photos
Long press the first photo to enter select mode. Tap all the photos from the trip. Use the date headers to quickly find Jun 29 – Jul 2 photos.
💡 Tip: Tap the date "Jun 29" header to select all photos from that day at once.
5
Tap Add → Done 🎉
All selected photos upload to the shared album. Depending on your internet speed, larger batches may take a minute.
1
Open the album in your browser
Go to photos.app.goo.gl/W1zCAE1NW9KFy8Qz9 ↗ and sign in with your Google account.
2
Click "Add photos" button
You'll see an "Add photos" button at the top right of the album page. Click it.
3
Choose your source
A dialog asks where to get photos from:
• "Select from computer" — drag and drop or browse your files
• "Select from Google Photos" — if photos are already backed up
4
Bulk select your photos
In the file picker, hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) and click each photo to select multiple. Or select the first, hold Shift, click the last to select a range.
💡 Tip: Sort your folder by date to find Jun 29 – Jul 2 photos quickly.
5
Upload & wait 🎉
Click Open/Upload. A progress bar shows the upload. Once done, all crew members can see your photos in the album immediately.
🔑
Wrong account?
Make sure you're signed into the same Google account that Dipankar granted access to. You can switch accounts in the top-right corner of Google Photos.
📶
On mobile data?
Use WiFi for bulk uploads — large photos can eat through data quickly. The VRBO WiFi password is nestuseful358.

Upload during the trip
Best to upload each evening so everyone can enjoy the day's photos together at the house.
