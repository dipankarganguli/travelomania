The Itinerary

1Day
Monday, June 29
Albany → Verona Beach → Clayton Waterfront
  • 10:00 AM
    Depart Albany Travel
    ~2 hrs 45 min to Verona Beach via I-90 W. Coordinate carpooling the night before — confirm who's in which car.
  • ~12:30 PM
    Lunch stop — Verona Beach State Park Picnic
    Beautiful lakeside park on Oneida Lake — great for a picnic lunch stretch. 📍 Verona Beach State Park, NY 13162 ↗ · Bring packed lunch from Albany or grab at a deli on the way. ~30 min stop.
  • ~3:00 PM
    Arrive Clayton — Explore the Waterfront & Riverwalk Explore
    📍 Clayton Riverwalk, Riverside Dr, Clayton NY ↗ · Stroll the waterfront, browse the shops on James St, take in the first views of the St. Lawrence. Grab coffee or a snack.
  • ~7:30 PM
    Sunset on the St. Lawrence 🌅 Scenic
    📍 Clayton Town Dock & Pier, Riverside Dr ↗ · Sun sets ~8:30 PM late June. The pier faces west — perfect golden light over the islands. Don't miss this on Day 1.
  • By 6:00 PM
    Check in — VRBO Stay
    40537 NY-12, Clayton NY · Host: Allison Elliott · 📞 315-405-3471 · WiFi: nestuseful358
    🛒 Grocery run: Clayton Shurfine Supermarket, 503 State St (5 min) — pick up BBQ supplies tonight!
  • Evening
    BBQ at the VRBO 🔥 Food
    Fire up the grill on arrival night — low key, get settled, let the kids run around. Assign tomorrow's breakfast duty.

2Day
Tuesday, June 30
Two Nations Tour · Boldt Castle · Antique Boat Museum
  • Breakfast
    Breakfast at the VRBO 🍳 Food
    Home cooked — assign duty the night before. Early start today, so keep it simple.
  • Morning
    Uncle Sam Two Nations Tour + Boldt Castle Tour
    📍 Uncle Sam Boat Tours, 44 James St, Clayton NY ↗
    🎟️ Tickets: usboattours.com ↗ — book in advance! Combo tour covers 40+ islands (US + Canada) and stops at Boldt Castle. Bring passports for the Canadian side. ~3.5 hrs. $32/person for castle entry.
  • Afternoon
    Antique Boat Museum Tour
    📍 750 Mary St, Clayton NY · ~7 min from VRBO ↗
    🎟️ Adults $15 · Kids under 12 free · abm.org ↗
    🕐 Hours: Daily 9 AM – 5 PM (May–Oct). World's largest collection of antique boats on the water. The kids will love the boathouse.
  • By 8:00 PM
    Back to VRBO 🏠
    Dinner on the way back or pick up food from Channelside / Wood Boat Brewery.

Trip Recap

The Story

Four days on the St. Lawrence River. Replace this with your post-trip recap — the highlights, unexpected moments, the food, the light on the water at 8 PM. This is what friends and family will read.

Day 1 was all about settling in. Clayton is one of those river towns that feels untouched by time — wooden docks, fishing boats, and a main street that smells like sunscreen and fried fish.

Day 2 we hit Boldt Castle — and somehow it exceeded expectations. George Boldt built a 120-room castle on Heart Island as a gift for his wife, but she died before it was finished. He never returned. Walking through it felt like stepping into a story that never got its ending.

Day 3 was Cedar Point. Write your own memories here — swimming, kayaking, what the St. Lawrence looked like in the July afternoon light.

Then it was over as fast as it began. July 2nd, packing the car, the drive home still replaying the highlights. Already talking about next year.
