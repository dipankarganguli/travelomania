2Day
Monday, June 30
Boldt Castle · Two Nation Tour · Sunset
  • Morning
    Boldt Castle Tour
    Heart Island, Alexandria Bay. Opens 10:30 AM. $32/person includes ferry + Yacht House. Download the app for the audio tour. ⭐ 4.8/5 (6,600+ reviews).
  • Afternoon
    Two Nation Tour Tour
    Departs Clayton dock. Cruises 40+ islands on both US & Canadian sides of the St. Lawrence. Bring passports! Departures usually 10 AM & 1 PM.
  • Lunch
    Waterfront lunch Food
    Plenty of options near the castle dock in Alexandria Bay, or head back to Clayton.
  • Sunset
    Clayton Waterfront 🌅 Scenic
    Riverside Drive. Sunset ~8:30 PM late June. The pier faces west — perfect golden hour light over the islands.

Trip Recap

The Story

Four days on the St. Lawrence River. Replace this with your post-trip recap — the highlights, unexpected moments, the food, the light on the water at 8 PM. This is what friends and family will read.

Day 1 was all about settling in. Clayton is one of those river towns that feels untouched by time — wooden docks, fishing boats, and a main street that smells like sunscreen and fried fish.

Day 2 we hit Boldt Castle — and somehow it exceeded expectations. George Boldt built a 120-room castle on Heart Island as a gift for his wife, but she died before it was finished. He never returned. Walking through it felt like stepping into a story that never got its ending.

Day 3 was Cedar Point. Write your own memories here — swimming, kayaking, what the St. Lawrence looked like in the July afternoon light.

Then it was over as fast as it began. July 2nd, packing the car, the drive home still replaying the highlights. Already talking about next year.
