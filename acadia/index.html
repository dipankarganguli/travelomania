Memories

Five days in Maine's crown jewel — granite peaks, crashing surf, popovers at Jordan Pond, and a sunset from Cadillac Mountain that stopped everyone mid-sentence.

It started with potato donuts in Portland. That's the kind of trip this was — the planning was meticulous (Dipankar's PPT had 22 slides), but the best moments were the unplanned ones: the kids piling into the car at golden hour, everyone suddenly quiet as the sun dropped behind the mountains.

Day 2 at Acadia was Ocean Path — Thunder Hole, Monument Cove, Boulder Beach strung together like beads on a 2-mile walk. Tanmay got the shots. Goutam found the lobster sliders. The rest of us just tried to keep up.

Jordan Pond on Day 3 was the flat hike everyone needed after the previous day's ambitions. The popovers at the Jordan Pond House — hollow, steaming, eaten with butter and jam on a lawn overlooking the water — were worth the entire drive to Maine.

Day 4: Beehive Trail. Not everyone made it to the top, but everyone has a story about it. Bass Harbor Lighthouse at the south tip of the island was the reward — painted against the evening sky, exactly as photogenic as every postcard promised.

Then it was over. July 1st, loading the cars, the kids already asleep. The kind of tired that only comes from doing something really right.

Food & Insider Tips

🏆 Must Try
Popovers at Jordan Pond House
Hollow muffins served with butter and jam on the lawn overlooking the pond. The most iconic food experience in Acadia. May need a reservation.
🏆 Must Try
Potato Donuts — Holy Donut
194 Park Ave, Portland. Potato donuts in multiple flavors. Worth stopping both ways. Congdon's is a good alternative.
🦞 Seafood
Lobster Sliders, Bar Harbor
Fresh Maine lobster on a soft roll. Buy it at any waterfront shack in Bar Harbor. Eat outside. Get two.
🫐 Local
Lowbush Blueberry Treats
Maine's wild blueberries are smaller and more intense than cultivated ones. Try the blueberry coffee cake and blueberry soda wherever you see them.

Practical Tips

Before You Go

🎟️
Reserve Cadillac Mountain
Book on Recreation.gov at least 2 days ahead. Search "Cadillac Summit Road Vehicle Reservation." Sunrise slots go fastest.
🌊
Check Tide Times
Thunder Hole: visit 1.5–2 hrs before high tide. Bar Island walk: within 1.5 hrs of low tide. Check tidesandcurrents.noaa.gov.
🚌
Free Island Explorer Shuttle
Hop-on hop-off bus covers the whole park. Download the Island Explorer app (select ANP). Saves parking headaches on busy days.
🛒
Walmart in Ellsworth
17 Myrick St, Ellsworth. Stock up on snacks, sunscreen, and drinks before entering the park area. Saves $$ vs Bar Harbor prices.
👟
Bring the Right Gear
Hiking shoes, tripod, binoculars, bug spray, sunscreen, water bottles, beach towel, bear spray on trail, camp chairs for summit.
📱
Download These Apps
NPS app, Shaka Guide (audio tour), Island Explorer (bus), myStop Mobile (real-time shuttle tracking).
