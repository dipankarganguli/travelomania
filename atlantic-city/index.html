Day by Day

The Itinerary

1Day
Day One
South Indian feast & a visit to the temple
  • Lunch
    Saravana Bhavan Food
    Kick things off with the legendary South Indian vegetarian spread — crispy dosas, fluffy idlis, filter coffee and thali. The perfect fuel before the temple.
  • Afternoon
    BAPS Swaminarayan Temple Temple
    Darshan and a slow walk around the stunning hand-carved mandir. Peaceful, breathtaking architecture — dress modestly and leave plenty of time to take it all in.
  • Evening
    Drive to Atlantic City Drive
    Cruise down toward the shore, check in, and catch the boardwalk lights coming on over the ocean.
2Day
Day Two
Atlantic City — boardwalk, beach & the strip
  • Morning
    Boardwalk stroll & the beach Views
    Start with a walk along the world-famous boardwalk, toes in the sand, and the Atlantic breeze. Grab saltwater taffy and don't skip the Steel Pier.
  • Afternoon
    Explore Atlantic City Fun
    Casinos and glittering resorts, the shops at The Playground, arcade games, and a bite along the way. Something for everyone in the crew.
  • Sunset
    Ocean sunset & head home Views
    One last look at the water as the lights take over, then the easy drive back — full bellies and a phone full of photos.
