Day by Day

The Itinerary

1Day
Saturday, August 8
Edison feast & the temple
  • 9:00 AM
    Start for Edison Drive
    Hit the road at 9 AM toward Edison, NJ — the heart of Central Jersey's Indian community along Oak Tree Road.
  • Lunch
    Lunch in Edison Food
    Take your pick of Oak Tree Road's best: Saravana Bhavan 📍 (South Indian veg) · Moghul 📍 · Chand Palace 📍 (pure veg) · Chowpatty 📍.
  • Afternoon
    BAPS Swaminarayan Akshardham, Robbinsville 📍 Temple
    Darshan and a slow walk around the breathtaking hand-carved mandir in Robbinsville — one of the largest in the world. Dress modestly and leave plenty of time to take it all in.
  • Evening
    Check in — Comfort Inn & Suites, Mt. Holly 📍 Stay
    Drive to Westampton and check in for the night — 2024 Route 541, RD 1, Mount Holly, NJ 08060. Rest up before the shore day.
2Day
Sunday, August 9
Wildwood boardwalk → Atlantic City
  • 8:00 AM
    Breakfast Food
    Breakfast at 8 AM, then check out and hit the road for the shore.
  • Morning
    Wildwood Boardwalk 📍 Boardwalk
    Drive to the famous Wildwood Boardwalk (3500 Boardwalk, Wildwood, NJ 08260) — rides, arcades, wide free beaches, and Morey's Piers. Plan to spend about 2 hours here.
  • Lunch
    Sandalwood Indian Kitchen 📍 Food
    Indian lunch just off the island in Rio Grande — about 10–15 min drive (~4 mi) from Wildwood beach. Directions from Wildwood beach ↗
  • Afternoon
    Check in — Caesars Atlantic City 📍 Stay
    Head back up the shore (~45 min) to Atlantic City and check in to Caesars, right on the boardwalk.
  • Evening
    Atlantic City Boardwalk 📍 Fun
    Stroll the world-famous boardwalk as the lights come on — Steel Pier, the shops at The Playground, arcades, saltwater taffy, and the ocean breeze.
3Day
Monday, August 10
Beach morning & head home
  • Morning
    Morning at the beach 📍 Beach
    Spend the morning on the Atlantic City beach — sand, surf, and one last dose of ocean before the drive back.
  • Lunch
    Indian lunch near the beach Food
    Top-rated Indian spots near the AC boardwalk: Tandoori Bites 📍 · Namaste 📍 · Cinnamon Indian Cuisine 📍.
  • 5:00 PM
    Head back home Drive
    Hit the road around 5 PM — full bellies, sandy shoes, and a phone full of photos.
